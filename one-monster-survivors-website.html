About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed roguelike game where you face endless waves of monsters. Inspired by popular games in the genre, our take offers unique characters, weapons, and monster designs to create a fresh experience.

The objective is simple: survive as long as possible while defeating monsters to gain experience and level up. Each level gained allows you to choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones, creating a unique build for every playthrough.

Whether you prefer a fast-paced action-oriented playstyle or a more strategic approach, Monster Survivors offers something for everyone. With simple controls but deep gameplay mechanics, you'll find yourself coming back for "just one more run" again and again!
