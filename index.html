Play Monster Survivors

Use WASD or arrow keys to move. Automatically attack nearby monsters and collect experience to level up. Choose new abilities as you progress!

Experience the Thrill of Survival

Monster Survivors drops you into a world overrun by hordes of monsters. Your goal is simple: survive as long as possible while defeating enemies, gaining experience, and unlocking powerful abilities.

Fast-Paced Action

Engage in non-stop combat against waves of monsters. Quick reflexes and strategic positioning are key to survival.

Roguelike Progression

Every run is unique with randomized power-ups. Gain experience, level up, and unlock powerful abilities.

Build Customization

Craft your own unique character build by choosing from a variety of abilities, weapons, and passive upgrades.
